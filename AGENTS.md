# Repository instructions

- Preserve the AI-authorship and unreviewed-code disclosure as the first line of `README.md` unless the repository owner explicitly asks to change it.
- Update `CHANGELOG.md` in the same change whenever work alters user-visible behaviour, supported data, schemas, provider contracts, compatibility, security, reliability, milestone status or a distributed release.
- Add ongoing work under `Unreleased`. When publishing a version, move the applicable entries into a dated version section and leave a fresh `Unreleased` section at the top.
- Keep changelog entries concise, factual and understandable to users. Do not silently rewrite previously published history; add a correction when necessary.
- Purely internal refactors and test-only changes may be grouped or omitted unless they affect behaviour, compatibility or confidence in a release.
